Required information to post:

If for some reason you decide to refuse the purchased goods, you can do this in accordance with the Law
of the Russian Federation "On the Protection of Consumer Rights" dated February 7, 1992 No. 2300-1.

Information not required for posting:

Return of goods is possible subject to the following conditions:

Return of goods of good quality.

__The Client has the right to refuse the ordered Goods at any time before receiving it, and after receiving
the Goods - within 7 (seven) days, not counting the day of purchase.
__The return of the Goods of good quality is possible if its presentation, consumer properties, as well as
a document confirming the fact and conditions of purchase of the said Goods are preserved.
__The Client does not have the right to refuse the Goods having individually defined properties if the
said Goods can be used exclusively by the Client acquiring it.
__If the Client refuses the Goods of good quality, the Seller must return to him the amount of money paid
by the consumer under the contract, except for the costs of the seller for the delivery of the returned
goods from the consumer, no later than 10 days from the date the consumer presents the relevant demand.

Return of goods of inadequate quality

A product of inadequate quality means a product that is not able to ensure its functional qualities due
to a significant drawback (with the presence of defects / defects).

The buyer, to whom the goods of inadequate quality were sold, if this was not agreed by the seller, has
the right to declare any of the following requirements at his choice:

__replacement for a product of a similar brand (model, article) or for the same product of another brand
(model, article) with a corresponding recalculation of the purchase price;

__proportional reduction of the purchase price;

__gratuitous elimination of defects in the goods or reimbursement of expenses for their correction by the
buyer or a third party;

__has the right to refuse to fulfill the contract and demand the return of the amount paid for the goods.

When returning technically complex goods of inadequate quality (the list of such goods was approved by
Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation of November 10, 2011 No. 924 “On approval of the list
of technically complex goods”), the Buyer has the right to demand:

__replacement for a product of a similar brand (model, article) or for the same product of another brand
(model, article) with a corresponding recalculation of the purchase price;

__refuse to fulfill the contract and demand a refund of the amount paid for the goods.

All of the above requirements for the return of goods of inadequate quality can be presented within the
following terms:

__The product has a warranty period - during the entire warranty period;

__The product does not have a warranty period - within a reasonable period, but not more than 2 years.

The online store has the right to refuse to exchange or return the goods if it considers that the
detected significant defect is the result of improper use of the goods.

In case of disagreement over the quality or the causes of defects, the online store has the right to
conduct an independent examination at its own expense. The buyer can participate in the examination and
challenge its decision in court. If, as a result of the examination, it is established that the defects
of the goods arose due to the fault of the Buyer, then the Buyer is obliged to reimburse the cost of the
examination to the online store, as well as the costs of transporting and storing the goods associated
with it.
